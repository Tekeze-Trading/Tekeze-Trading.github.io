Our Services in Detail

Our objective is to thoroughly understand client needs, develop a comprehensive execution plan, and deliver actionable recommendations for sustainable resource management.

  • Study, design, and supervision of hydropower, irrigation, water supply, and drainage projects.
  • Water resources development, watershed management, soil and water conservation.
  • Surface and groundwater assessments, well design, drilling supervision, pump test analysis.
  • Hydrologic and hydrogeologic modeling.
  • Environmental and socio-economic impact assessments (ESIA).
  • Preparation of tender documents for water resource projects.
  • Reconnaissance, feasibility, and detailed studies for roads, bridges, airways, and railways.
  • Geotechnical investigations and topographic surveys.
  • Pavement condition analysis and design.
  • Comprehensive hydrology and hydraulic studies for transport infrastructure.
  • Contract administration, supervision, and tender document preparation.
  • Geoenvironmental and geohazard impact assessments.
  • Natural hazard modeling using GIS and remote sensing technologies.
  • Geotechnical subsurface investigations for various structures.
  • Foundation design and analysis.
  • Landslide hazard assessment and mitigation design.
  • Detailed geological mapping and construction material assessments.

Irrigation

Amhara Region Irrigation Network

Transportation

Addis Ababa Interchange Design

Geo-Environmental

Rift Valley Geothermal Study
